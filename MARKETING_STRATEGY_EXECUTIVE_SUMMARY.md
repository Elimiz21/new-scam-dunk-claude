## üéØ Key Tactics

### Awareness Stage
- SEO-optimized blog content (target: 100+ keywords by Month 6)
- Social media content (TikTok, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn)
- HARO responses (5/week ‚Üí 2-3 media mentions/month)
- Influencer partnerships (20 micro-influencers by Month 3)
- Product Hunt launch (Top 5 goal)

### Consideration Stage
- Lead magnets (PDFs, checklists, guides)
- Email nurture sequences (7 emails over 14 days)
- Educational content series ("Scam School", "Protect Your Parents")
- Social proof (testimonials, case studies, user count)

### Conversion Stage
- Free trial (7 days, no credit card initially)
- Landing page optimization (A/B testing)
- Referral program (1 month free for referrer + referee)
- Launch promotions (50% off first month)

### Retention Stage
- Weekly scam alerts (email + push)
- Monthly protection reports
- Community building (Discord, Facebook groups)
- Re-engagement campaigns (inactive users)
