# Scam Dunk Executive Summary

- **Product**: AI‑powered, multi‑signal scam diagnostic with a lead focus on price movement/manipulation detection; web live, mobile in ~2 weeks. Diagnostic, not advisory.
- **Goal (6 months)**: 1M visitors, 50K users, 10K subscribers. Zero/lean‑budget, organic‑first.
- **Positioning**: Fastest multi‑signal scam screener with market‑manipulation detection; privacy‑first, senior‑friendly.
- **ICP**: Retail traders (18–35), caregiver protectors (35–60), privacy‑conscious consumers (25–45).
- **Strategy**: Organic SEO + creator‑led social + referral program; data‑story PR; light retargeting.
- **Content Pillars**: Price manipulation explainers; scam exposure teardowns; weekly Scam Watch; live “is this legit?” demos.
- **Offers**: Free 3 lifetime scans (+referral scan credits). Paid Starter $9.99, Pro $19.99; annual 2 months free; Family add‑on.
- **90‑Day Objectives**: 200k–400k sessions; >35% visit→signup on scan flows; 12–18% trial→paid; 30‑day paid retention ≥70%.
- **Automation**: Welcome/activation/churn/referral email flows; in‑app nudges; dynamic content testing; lead scoring.
- **Timeline**: Week 0–1 foundation; Weeks 2–3 public beta; Weeks 4–5 launch; Months 2–6 programmatic SEO + community + creators.
- **Risks/Mitigation**: Claims compliance (diagnostic language, disclaimers), brigading (SOP + rate limits), content velocity (AI pipeline), API costs (caching/tiers).

> Compliance note: Use “informational diagnostics,” avoid guarantees; include disclosures on all acquisition surfaces.
